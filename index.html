Welcome to my homepage!

I'm a cosmologist and currently a KIPAC fellow at the Kavli Institute for Particle Astrophysics and Cosmology (KIPAC) at Stanford University and SLAC National Accelerator and Laboratory. My research focuses primarily on the connections between fundamental physics at microscopic scales and the distribution of structures on the largest scales we can access in the Universe.

I received my undegraduate degree in Physics from St. Stephen's College, in Delhi, India.
